About ResurgeJustice

ResurgeJustice is a nonprofit initiative committed to helping formerly incarcerated individuals rebuild their lives through employment, skill development, mentorship, and access to legal and community resources.

Our Mission

We strive to break down barriers and empower justice-impacted individuals with the tools they need to reintegrate into society. Our platform connects returning citizens to job opportunities, personal development programs, and a network of mentors and advocates who believe in second chances.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone — regardless of their past — is given the opportunity to thrive, contribute meaningfully, and build a stable future. ResurgeJustice believes in dignity, inclusion, and the power of community-led transformation.

What We Offer

  • Job Listings: Curated roles from employers who support second-chance hiring.
  • Mentorship: Personalized guidance from professionals and advocates who understand the journey.
  • Skill Development: Resources for building resumes, learning new trades, and preparing for success.
  • Legal & Community Support: Access to reentry services, housing help, and legal aid organizations.

Why It Matters

1 in 3 Americans has a criminal record. Many face lifelong stigma and barriers — especially in employment. At ResurgeJustice, we believe a mistake shouldn't define a person forever. With support, opportunity, and community, reentry can be a launchpad — not a setback.

Together, we’re not just reducing recidivism — we’re reigniting hope.
