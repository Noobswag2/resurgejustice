About ResurgeJustice

ResurgeJustice is a nonprofit initiative committed to helping formerly incarcerated individuals rebuild their lives through employment, skill development, and mentorship opportunities.

Our Mission

We strive to break down barriers and empower people with the tools and resources they need to successfully reintegrate into society. Our platform connects individuals with job listings, mentorship, legal aid, and community resources tailored to support second chances.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone, regardless of their past, is given the opportunity to thrive, contribute, and build a better future.
